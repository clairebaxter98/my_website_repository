Margarita


Glass:Martini Glass

Ingredients:
  • 50ml tequila
  • 1 1/2 tbsp lime juice, plus extra for the glass
  • 1 tbsp triple sec
  • Salt and slice of lime, to serve
Method:
  1. Put a little salt on a saucer and wipe the rim of the martini glass with lime juice
  2. Turn the glass upside down in the salt and twist to coat
  3. Stir the ingredients and a little ice together, or put them into a cocktail shaker to combine
  4. Strain into a chilled martini glass. Serve with a slice of lime




Tequila Sunrise


Glass:Highball

Ingredients:
  • 2 tsp grenadine
  • Ice
  • 50ml tequila
  • 1 tbsp triple sec
  • 1 large orange, juiced
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
Method:
  1. Pour the grenadine into a tall glass and set aside
  2. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice and add the tequila, triple sec and fruit juices. Shake until the outside of the shaker feels cold
  3. Add a few cubes of ice to the serving glass then doubly strain the cocktail into it, trying not to disturb the grenadine layer
  4. Add more ice if needed to fill the glass




Vampiro


Glass:Small wine glass

Ingredients:
  • 40ml Blanco tequila
  • 10ml Mescal
  • 20ml Passata
  • 10ml Lime juice
  • 5ml Lemon juice
  • 5ml grenadine
  • 10ml Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 dashes Tabasco
  • Smoked sea salt, finely ground
Method:
  1. Chill a small wine glass then brush a little water on the rim and dip into a saucer of sea salt
  2. Put all the ingredients in a cocktail shaker and fill with ice
  3. Stir until the shaker feels cold on the outsidea, strain into the glass
