Mojito


Glass:Highball

Ingredients:
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • Small handful of mint leaves, plus extra sprig to serve
  • 60ml white rum
  • Crushed ice
  • Soda water, to taste
  • Lime wedge to garnish
Method:
  1. Muddle the lime juice, sugar and mint leaves in a small jug, crushing the mint as you go
  2. Pour into a glass and add a handful of ice
  3. Pour over the rum, stirring with a long-handled spoon
  4. Top up with soda water, garnish with mint and lime




Daiquiri


Glass:Martini Glass

Ingredients:
  • 50ml white rum
  • 35ml fresh lime juice
  • 4 tsp sugar syrup (gomme)
  • 1 lime wedge
Method:
  1. Place the rum, lime juice and sugar syrup into a cocktail shaker and shake well to combine
  2. Pour into a Martini glass and serve garnished with a wedge of lime
Try adding different fruit purees to the mixture to switch up the flavours. Summer fruits and berries work well




Pina Colada


Glass:Highball

Ingredients:
  • 60ml white rum
  • 120ml pineapple juice
  • 60ml coconut cream
  • Wedge of pineapple to garnish
Method:
  1. Pulse all the ingredients along with a hadful of ice in a blender until smooth
  2. Pour into a tall glass and garnish as you like




Dark and Stormy


Glass:Highball

Ingredients:
  • 50ml dark rum
  • 25ml fresh lime juice
  • 2 dashes of Angostura bitters
  • 5ml sugar syrup
  • 100ml ginger beet
  • Wedge of lime to garnish
Method:
  1. Place all the ingredients in a glass and cover with lots of ice
  2. Stir gently and garnish with a lime wedge
Try adding orange bitters instead to give more spicy, marmalade undertones
